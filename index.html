Our Story

Once upon a NYC night,
two kids from Miami met for drinks on a chilly night in November 2011. Vivian liked Dan immediately. Dan was oblivious. So began a six-month saga of hanging out and not kissing. Finally, Dan caught on and asked Vivian out on a date. Vivian wore a red skirt and cheetah pumps. Dan took her out to Cube Sushi. Dan was awkward. Vivian felt a mix of pena-ajena and butterflies. Dan kissed Vivian for the first time. Vivian discovered Dan was not always awkward. Dan discovered Vivian was weired & liked it. Soon they were inseperable. After two years of dating they decided to move to Brooklyn together. A year and a half later, Dan proposed to Vivian in the rain at her father’s house. Vivian said "YES!".
